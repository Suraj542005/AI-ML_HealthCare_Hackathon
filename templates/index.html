About Real-Time Object Detection

Real-time object detection involves using algorithms and models, such as YOLO (You Only Look Once), to identify and locate objects within video frames instantly. This technology is applied in various fields such as surveillance, autonomous driving, and robotics to enhance decision-making processes and improve safety and efficiency.
